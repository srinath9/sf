About Us

Spring Fest is the annual social and cultural festival of IIT Kharagpur ,the pioneer of these elite institutions. Currently in its 55th edition, Spring Fest has been celebrating the true spirit of youth for over five decades, growing bigger and better with every passing year. A perfect blend of fun and frolic, of excitement and ecstasy, of devotion and dedication, Spring Fest is a celebration with a responsibility. A splendid platform for the spectacular display of the diverse talents of today's youth, Spring Fest always has an underlying social theme.
Spring Fest is arguably the largest social and cultural festival in Asia and is what brings alive the 2,100 acre IIT Kharagpur campus on the Republic Day weekend every year. It is that time of the year when students from all over come down to Kharagpur to participate in the plethora of novel events and to witness the biggest stars from India and abroad perform.
Spring Fest-IIT Kharagpur is proud to have been a defining platform for some of the most accomplished artists in diverse fields as music, dance, drama, debate, quizzes and photography, many of whom have come back a few years later as stars. It is one of IIT Kharagpur's most cherished traditions and is renowned for the varied experiences, sweet memories and exciting stories that it leaves everyone with.
