Submit Your Site

Videos

Submit your site to be considered for the Adobe Muse customer showcase, Site of the Day, Design Center, or as a Muse Jam Session topic. Your submission must meet the following criteria:

  • Successful Muse sites will exhibit strong visual design and usability, interesting and/or timely content, and innovative use of Muse features.
  • All work must be original (no plagiarized images, text, or other content).
  • Adobe Muse must have been used to create the project.
  • The website must not require the viewer to register. (If registration is required and you would still like to be considered, please submit a user name and password that all viewers can use.)
  • No pornography will be accepted.

Sites that include phone layouts are more likely to be accepted to Muse Site of the Day.
