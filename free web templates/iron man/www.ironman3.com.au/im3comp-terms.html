To enter “Iron Man 3 Code Competition” (“Competition”), you must, during the Entry Period:

  1. Purchase a specially marked copy of Iron Man 3 on DVD or Blu-Ray from a participating Australian retail store and look out for code 1 within the DVD or Blu-Ray cover ( “Code 1”); and/or
  2. Watch the “Thor: The Dark World” trailer on the Iron Man 3 website located at www.ironman3.com.au and identify code 2 as per the instruction provided on the Competition page found at www.ironman3.com.au( “Code 2”); and/or
  3. Log on to TLC Electronics Facebook page located at www.facebook.com/pages/TCL-Electronics-Australia-Pty-Ltd/194537067250784 and look out for code 3 ( “Code 3”); and
  4. log on to the Iron Man 3 website located at www.ironman3.com.au and click through to the Competition page;
  5. enter Code 1 and/or Code 2 and/or Code 3;
  6. complete all of the required data entry fields on the entry form; and
  7. submit your details through filling out and submitting the entry form, (collectively, the “Entry”).
