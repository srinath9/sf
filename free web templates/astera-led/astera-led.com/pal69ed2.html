Wireless Wallwasher

product code: AL6 series

Our wireless Wallwasher is made for uncomplicated event illumination and decorative lighting. Simply place it down next to a wall, switch it on and the wall will be transformed into a wash of colors.

Technical Specification

  • Illuminants:

    AL6-S 96 bullet LED (24xR, 24xG, 24xB, 24xW) = 12W RGB power
    AL6-M 192 bullet LED (48xR, 48xG, 48xB, 48xW) = 24W RGB power
    AL6-L 384 bullet LED (96xR, 96xG, 96xB, 96xW) = 48W RGB power

  • Beam Angle:

    15 degree

  • Diffuser Lens:

    Included, can be easily attached

  • Battery:

    Built-in Li-Ion-Polymer battery for up to 24h operation

  • Power Input:

    110-240VAC, 50/60Hz

  • Wireless Module:

    868MHz receiver with up to 300m range (US version: 902-928MHz / 330yd)

  • DMX Capable:

    Over integrated DMX sockets or W-DMX with ART3 (Wireless DMX Transmitter)

  • Housing:

    Linear aluminum housing and clear PMMA cover

  • Dimensions:

    AL6-S: 350mm x 98mm x 66mm (L13.8” x H3.7” x W2.6”)
    AL6-M: 570mm x 98mm x 66mm (L22.4” x H3.7” xW2.6”)
    AL6-L: 1070mm x 98mm x 66mm (L42.1” x H3.7”x W2.6”)

  • Weight:

    1kg (2.2lb)
    2 kg (4.4lb)
    4 kg (8.8lb)

  • Environment:

    Indoor & dry outdoor

  • Extras:

    Gamma correction, temperature compensation, color calibration

  • Included Accessories:

    Power cord for built-in power supply unit, diffuser lens

  • Suitable Applications:

    Under table/ furniture lighting, effect lighting, wall washing, beach or tree lighting

Main Benefits

  • Rechargeable battery

  • Remote-controlled

  • 12-48W LED power

  • Wireless DMX

  • Flicker-free operation

  • Stand-alone operation

Compatible Controllers

  • ARC1

  • ARC2

  • ART3

  • AsteraBox™
