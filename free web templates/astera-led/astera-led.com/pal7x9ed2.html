Wireless Water-resistant Spotlight

product code: AL7-X

The wireless spotlight is the most advanced and most powerful wireless spotlight on the market. You can launch complex lighting programs and set the right mood for your events and stage performances.

Technical Specification

  • Illuminants:

    AL7-XL 12 x high-power RGB LEDs, 6 x high-power white LEDs
    AL7-XXL 24 x high-power RGB LEDs, 12 x high-power white LEDs

  • Total LED Power:

    AL7-XL 54W
    AL7-XXL 108W

  • Luminous emittance:

    AL7-XL 1330 Lux @ 2m
    AL7-XXL 2360 Lux @ 2m

  • Beam Angle:

    25 degree (achieved through lenses)

  • Diffuser Lens:

    120 degree lens sold separately, can be easily attached

  • Battery:

    Built-in Samsung battery for up to 24h operation

  • Charger Input:

    110-240VAC, 50/60Hz

  • Charger Output:

    AL7-XL: 29VDC, 2.5A
    AL7-XXL: 48VDC, 2.5A

  • Wireless Module:

    868MHz receiver, up to 300m range (US version: 902-928MHz, up to 330yd)

  • DMX Capable:

    W-DMX with Astera Wireless DMX Transmitter, or wired DMX over XLR-Adapter

  • Control Pad:

    3 line blue/white display with 5 buttons for independent operation & DMX setting

  • Housing:

    Water-resistant cast iron housing

  • Dimensions:

    L18cm x H28cm x W17cm (L7.1” x H11.0” x W6.7”)

  • Weight:

    AL7-XL 4176g (9.20lb)

  • AL7-XXL

    4179g (9.21lb)

  • Environment:

    Indoor & outdoor (IP54)

  • Extras

    Backside display and buttons, gamma correction, temperature compensation

  • Included Accessories:

    External power supply unit,

  • Suitable Applications:

    Stage lighting, theatre lighting, event lighting, club lighting, outdoor lighting
