SpotMax™

product code: AX10

Representing our X-Series, the next generation in wireless lighting, the SpotMax™ spotlight brings together years of knowledge in wireless lighting. The SpotMax™ adds amber LEDs for improved color saturation. It can be controlled by all of our remote controllers, our wireless DMX transmitter and also by third party W-DMX controllers.

Technical Specification

  • Illuminants:

    9 x 15W RGBAW Philips LEDs

  • Total LED Power:

    135W

  • Luminous Flux:


    3,350 lm at 4000K
    3,150 lm at 6500K
    3,100 lm at 7500K

  • Illuminance:

    10,350 lx at 2m
    4,700 lx at 3m - 1,800 lx at 5m

  • CRI:

    92 at 3000K (60%-70% intensity)

  • Strobe:

    0-25Hz

  • Beam Angle:

    13° (achieved through Khatod lenses)

  • Battery:

    Built-in Samsung battery


  • Battery Lifetime:

    70% capacity (after 300 charging cycles)

  • Operation:

    5-20 hours: 50,000 hours

  • Charging Time::

    5-20 hours (depending on ambient temp.)

  • Working Voltage:

    Input 110-240V, 50/60Hz

  • Power Consumption:

    170W (maximum)

  • Wireless Module:

    868MHz receiver (US:902-928MHz)
    2.4GHz wireless module

  • Range:

    300m (330yards)

  • DMX Interface:


    CRMX Wireless DMX from LumenRadio
    Wireless DMX (with ART3 Transmitter) and supports W-DMX

  • Power Connectors:

    2 (Neutrik Powercon True1 In & Out)

  • Housing:

    Cast aluminium, Tempered glass

  • IP Rating:

    IP65

  • Relative Humidity:

    0-100% (non-condensing)

  • Operating Temperature:

    0°-40°C (32°-104°F)

  • Weight:

    8.14kg (18lbs)

  • Dimensions:

    L279mm x H296mm x W253mm
    L10.9” x H11.6” x W9.9”

  • Environment:

    Indoor / Outdoor

  • Features:


    TruColor™ Calibration, Temperature compensation,
    Dynamic power-boost, Auto-white,
    Auto-amber, Dual antennas

  • Included Accessories:

    Power Cord, 2 x Diffusor Sheets

    Charging Case (holds 4)
