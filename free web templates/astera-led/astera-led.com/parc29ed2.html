RF Remote Controller

product code: ARC2

The RF Remote Control is our standard handheld wireless controller. It is used to change colors, launch programs, group together several of our wireless lights, arrange lights within groups and control third-party products too. All that is needed is the ARC2 and our lights, no other transmitter/receiver devices are needed.

Technical Specification

  • Battery:

    2 x 1.5V (AA type)

  • Protocols:

    Astera Wireless

  • Display:

    2 lines + 4 symbols

  • Control Limitation:

    Controls an unlimited amount of wireless Astera products

  • DMX Capability:

    Wireless DMX with Astera Wireless DMX Transmitter ART3

  • Wireless Module:

    868MHz sender/receiver (US version: 902-928MHz sender/receiver)

  • Range:

    300m (330yd)

  • Relative Humidity:

    0-95％

  • Operational Temp:

    5 – 40°C

  • Unit Size:

    L183mm x W62mm x H31mm (L7.2” x W2.4” x H1.2”)

  • Weight:

    0.160kg (0.35lb)

  • Housing Material:

    Plastic

  • Functions:

    Color mixing, Color calibration, Group addressing, Chain addressing, Type addressing, Serial Number addressing
