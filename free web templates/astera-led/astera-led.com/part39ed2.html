Wireless DMX Transmitter

product code: ART3

Our wireless DMX Receiver & Transmitter is a versatile product that can be used in several ways to form large wireless lighting setups. Use it as a basic DMX transmitter to link a DMX controller and its lights or connect it to third-party DMX lights and use our RF Remote Control to control them.

Technical Specification

  • Working Voltage:

    Input-110-240V, 50/60Hz

  • Battery:

    N/A

  • Output Socket:

    2 x XLR 3-pole (DMX in & out)

  • DMX Capability:

    DMX-512 in/out

  • Channels:

    512

  • Wireless Module:

    868MHz sender/receiver (US version: 902-928MHz sender/receiver)

  • Range:

    300m (330yd)

  • Control Pad:

    5 multi-functional buttons and LCD display for basic operation

  • Environment:

    Indoor and dry outdoor

  • Unit Size:

    L160mm x W105mm x H50mm (L6.3” x W4.1” x H2.0”)

  • Weight:

    0.850kg (1.9lb)

  • Casting Material:

    Aluminum

  • Modes:

    Sending, receiving, transmitting

  • Functions:

    Several DMX Tabs, 1 channel per color or launching complex programs, can control hundreds of fixtures, quickly choose DMX input address.

  • Included Accessories:

    Power cord for the integrated power supply
