Wireless Operation

All of our LED fixtures have a wireless receiver and battery included inside their housing. This enables an easy and cordless setup and is especially attractive for event decoration, stage lighting and other temporary ambient lighting installations. Our wireless lights are battery powered, which translates into shorter setup times and less manpower needed to set up your events, which will significantly reduce costs. The lights are powered by Lithium-Manganese-Nickel-Cobalt batteries, which is far superior to the average Lithium-Ion batteries that are so readily available from other companies. To reach our runtime standard of 5-20 hours, the X-Series uses mixed-cell Samsung batteries to support the high-brightness, power-consuming Cree LED chips. Every wireless fixture has enough battery to run for 5-20 hours on a full charge (depends on brightness and colors).

Our radio-frequency AsteraBox™/AsteraApp™ wireless controller is designed for effective event illumination. It is possible to build groups, sets and target lights within minutes, mix any color you can imagine and store complex color-changing effects as quick buttons for regular usage. Three types of remote controllers are available, from low-cost versions to Android applications. We circle around the 868MHz~915MHz band (soon to be 2.4GHZ), which improves ability of the radio-frequency control system and finds the optimal band for the situation. This means our lights can be controlled over a distance of 300 meters.

TruColor™ Calibration

Astera’s X-Series offers automatic TruColor™ Calibration, meaning that the difference in light colors across all batches is not noticeable to the human eye. We can match a Lee filter more accurately than any other light in this price range. In numbers, Astera’s X-series can display warm white light with an accuracy of within 100 kelvin.

Astera only uses ultra-bright and highly efficient light sources from reliable brands such as Cree and Philips. These LEDs maximize battery runtime and minimize heat emission. In addition to our RGBW modules, the SpotMax™ also includes amber LEDs, which improves the efficiency of color mixing and the vibrancy of pastel colors due to broadening the light spectrum. Adding the amber light also allows more accurate color reflection on yellow/brown/orange surfaces. With a standard yellow light shone onto an orange surface, the surface would be darkened, but with the RGBWA LEDs, the surface will reflect more accurately the color being shone.

Compact, Lightweight, Revolutionary

Design

The X-Series are all IP65 waterproof, and are also made of heat-conductive aluminium, making them more efficient than the previous range. They also contain very compact and reliable Samsung batteries, allowing our lights to hold more energy for the same size. These improvements are edging Astera closer to the perfection that we strive to achieve.

The X-Series will also offer a mesh network, which basically means that the lights will synchronize and communicate with each other. This in turn boosts range up to 1km. Furthermore, the AsteraBox™/AsteraApp™ controller will be able to automatically detect the battery status of all lights within a 1km radius. Other detection statuses are being implemented and tested like a theft and movement detection. These have been an on-going problem for event organizers, and now Astera has the answer.

Versatility, Consistency and

Usability

Astera offers a more stable and reliable wireless DMX solution by partnering with Lumen Radio. This means that users can control our X-Series range through CRMX wireless DMX transmitters, which is secure, wide-reaching and waterproof.

Also, all of our wireless event fixtures are suitable outdoors due to their cordless design. While some lights are only splash-proof and should be taken inside during a rain shower, others are fully waterproof for permanent outdoor use (see AX3, AX10).

Inside the box you will find a charger and a diffuser which is everything you will need to get your lighting started. Astera wireless technology does not require a router, gateway or a computer to operate them. Additionally, we have a large number of centerpieces and decorative objects available to be used with the Astera range of wireless lights. A small number of fixtures combined with several different objects provide a cost-effective way to decorate and maintain variety at events.
