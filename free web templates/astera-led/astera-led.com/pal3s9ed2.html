Mini Lightdrop™

product code: AL3-S

Our Mini Lightdrop™ is the perfect solution for centerpiece illumination. The Mini Lightrop™ can be set to 10 different colors and 6 different effects, all customizable in brightness and speed.

Technical Specification

  • LEDs:

    16 bullet LEDs (4xR, 4xG, 4xB, 4xW) = 1.7W RGB power

  • Max LED Power:

    2W

  • Beam Angle:

    15 degree

  • Battery:

    Built-in Li-Ion-Polymer battery

  • Battery runtime:

    up to 8 hours in white, up to 24 hours in red

  • Charger Input:

    110-240VAC, 50/60Hz

  • Charger Output:

    5VDC, 0.5A

  • Wireless Module:

    Infra-Red receiver

  • Material:

    Nylon, fiberglass reinforced and clear PMMA cover

  • Dimensions:

    Ø 59mm x H 43,5mm (Ø 2.3” x H 1.7”)

  • Weight:

    0,140kg (0.3lb)

  • Environment:

    Indoor & Outdoor (splash-proof)

Main Benefits

  • Rechargeable battery

  • Remote-controlled

  • Bright 2W LED power

  • Wireless DMX

  • Velcro® underside

  • Up to 20 hours life

  • Designed for Table Centerpiece illumination

Compatible Controllers

  • ARC1

  • ARC2

  • ART3

  • AsteraBox™

Accessories
