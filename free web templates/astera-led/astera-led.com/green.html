Energy Efficient Lighting

The Astera Green Lighting range provides energy-efficient LED lighting for normal everyday situations, and is the future of consumer lighting, leaving behind the wasteful and inefficient incandescent and compact fluorescent lighting (CFL). Our LED lights are long-lasting, cost-effective, more efficient, and mercury-free.


By combining more efficient LED lighting and networking, today’s smart street lighting yields significant energy and operational savings. While migrating to LEDs cuts energy costs, networking the lights boosts your operational savings. Astera’s smart lighting solutions enable you to reduce energy costs by migrating to networked LEDs, delivering 65% energy savings, and increase operational savings to 40% from smart dimming and lower runtimes.
