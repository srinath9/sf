Management Team

  • Norbert Ernst CEO Germany Norbert’s vision as an electrical engineer working in the LED lighting industry is what made Astera the best in the industry. He is still very much focused on product innovation and creative technologies, as well as our corporate strategy, and he is the driving force behind Astera’s revolutionary status.

  • Laura Zhang General Manager China Laura is responsible for the day-to-day operations of production. These include effective planning, delegating, coordinating, staffing, decision making and managing both revenue and cost elements of the company’s income statement to achieve the profit-making potential of Astera.

  • Sebastian Bückle Sales & Marketing Director Germany Sebastian is responsible for Astera’s marketing implementation, brand identity, market expansion and coordinating the sales team. His knowledge of Astera’s product range is unparalleled, and you will often see him at exhibitions and trade shows. He strongly believes in listening to the customer and you cannot go far wrong.

  • Balazs Maar Chief Financial Officer Hungary Balazs is responsible for the financial side of the business. Working closely with the CEO, Balazs briefs on financial opportunities, comprises the annual financial statements, and determines budgeting statuses

  • Hanan Yariv Technical Director Israel Hanan’s background as a programmer and his leadership qualities make him a first-class Technical Director. Hanan manages all areas of communication design, interface construction, testing and debugging our many systems, including our ERP system & our AsteraTouch™ technology.

  • Simon Canins R&D Director Germany Simon leads the team that provides the innovative spark at Astera. He is responsible for the design and development of new product concepts, as well as the leadership of the R&D team. Simon previously worked as a lighting technician in Munich’s top nightclubs and therefore has a solid background in the lighting industry, and is able to maintain Astera’s pioneering reputation.

  • Luis Arguello Operations Manager Mexico Luis is our operations manager and is responsible for the overall management and vision of Astera’s production, engineering and quality departments. Luis sets and governs manufacturing and quality processes, and implements procedures to ensure our quality standards.
