Lightdrop™

product code: AX3

Our Lightdrop™ is the most advanced LED spotlight for event lighting. It is the most compact, wireless spotlight on the market, and can be attached to walls, ceilings or simply placed inside our decorative polyethylene objects.

Technical Specification

  • Item Codes:

    AX3
    AX3-CRMX (includes Lumen Radio CRMX)

  • Total LED Power:

    15W (1 x RGBW CREE LED Chip)

  • Luminous Flux:

    850 lm on 6500K White

  • Illuminance:

    760 lx at 2m
    350 lx at 3m

  • Strobe:

    0-25Hz

  • Beam Angle:

    13° (achieved through Khatod lens)

  • Battery:

    Built-in Samsung battery

  • Battery Lifetime:

    70% capacity (after 300 charging cycles)

  • Operation:

    5-20 hours

  • LED Lifetime:

    50,000 hours

  • Charging Time

    5-7 hours (depending on ambient temp)

  • Working Voltage:

    Input 110-240V, 50/60Hz

  • Input Current:

    2.5A at 5V

  • Wireless Module:

    868MHz receiver (US: 902-928MHz)
    2.4GHz wireless module

  • Range:

    300m (330yds)

  • DMX Interface:

    Wireless DMX (with ART3 Transmitter)

  • Optional DMX:

    CRMX Wireless DMX from Lumen Radio
    also supports W-DMX

  • Housing:

    Polyamide / Polystyrene

  • IP Rating:

    IP65

  • Relative Humidity:

    0-100% (non-condensing)

  • Operating Temp:

    0°-40°C (32°-104°F)

  • Weight:

    0.430kg (0.9lbs)

  • Dimensions:

    L119mm x H59mm x W110mm
    L5.1” x H2.3” x W4.3”

  • Environment:

    Indoor / Outdoor

  • Features:

    TruColor™ Calibration, Temperature
    compensation, Dynamic power-boost

  • Included Accessories:

    Charger, 30° Flood & 120° Diffusor Sheet,
    Hooks, Bracket, Feet (x4), Rubber Plug

  • Also Available:

    17°x46° Wallwash Diffusor, Diffusor Dome, Truss Clamp

Main Benefits

  • Twice as bright as the AL3-M

  • Rechargeable Samsung battery

  • Remote-controlled

  • Ultra-bright 15W Cree LED

  • CRMX Wireless DMX

  • Magnetic underside

Compatible Controllers

  • ARC1

  • ARC2

  • ART3

  • AsteraBox™

  • CRMX Nova TX
