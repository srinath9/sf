BULBS

Our LED Bulbs are extremely energy efficient compared to CFB (compact fluorescent bulbs) and incandescent bulbs. LED bulbs typically use only 2-10 watts of electricity, which is 30% of the wattage used by CFB and only 3% of incandescent bulbs. Also, due to their long-life, LED bulbs save on maintenance and replacement costs (LEDs last up to ten times as long as CFB, and hundreds of times longer than incandescent bulbs).


An added bonus is that because of LED Bulbs’ low power requirement, they can be easily powered by practical sources such as solar panels. LED bulbs are also more efficient in producing light as they do not cause heat build-up (heat is dissipated via the aluminium sinks). LEDs produce approximately 3.4 BTUs (British Thermal Units) per hour, compared to 85 for incandescent bulbs.
