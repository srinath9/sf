English: This category is for spoken versions of Wikipedia articles. Please also add spoken to an appropriate topic.

Suggested naming convention for spoken articles: language-Name-article.ogg - for example, en-Evolution-article.ogg or de-Erkenntnistheorie-article.ogg.
