This category contains images with the {{Non-free frame}} cleanup tag. These images contain nonessential decorative elements such as a frame that are not known to be public domain or available under a free license. In particular, images marked with {{PD-Art}} may not include three-dimensional frame designs, because we do not have permission from the photographer. These elements should be removed.
