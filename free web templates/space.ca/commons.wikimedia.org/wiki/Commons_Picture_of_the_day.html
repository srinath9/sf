These are images selected for display on the Main Page as the Picture of the day. Publishing them on this page allows users to translate the captions into other languages.

As of 1 July 2007, all Pictures of the Day are chosen among the Featured pictures of Commons. Featured pictures are the finest images on Wikimedia Commons and are selected by consensus on Commons:Featured picture candidates. For instructions on how to add a Picture of the Day, see Commons:Picture of the day/Instructions.
