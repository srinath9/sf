Mallar relaterade till Sverige[edit]

Mallarna är främst för

  • vissa ofta använda svenska verk som är fria att använda för att deras upphovsrätt gått ut och
  • vissa verk av myndigheter, som aldrig fått upphovsrättsskydd eller som släppts fria av myndigheten ifråga.

{{PD-Sweden-photo}} nedan är för äldre svenska foton vars upphovsrätt gått ut (märk skillnaden mellan fotografiska verk och fotografiska bilder).

{{Insignia-Sweden}} nedan är en varningsskylt för svenska heraldiska vapenbilder: vapnen skyddas av lagstiftning oberoende av upphovsrätten. Det är bara vad gäller den senare som vapnen är under fri licens.

Finska fotografiska bilder[edit]

  • {{PD-Finland50}} – för finska fotografiska bilder som inte längre omfattas av upphovsrätt enligt finsk lag. Observera skillnaden till fotografiska verk, diskutera vid behov på Bybrunnen eller på Wikipedias Bildfrågor.


Public domainPublic domainfalsefalse
This photograph is in the public domain, because either 50 years has elapsed from the year of creation or the photograph was first published before 1966. The §49a of the Finnish copyright law of 2005 specifies that photographs not considered to be "works of art" become public domain 50 years after they were created. The 50 years from creation protection period came into force in 1991. Before that the protection period was 25 years from the year of first publication according to the §16 of the law of protection of photographs of 1961. Material already released to public domain according to the 1961 law remains in public domain, and therefore all photographs (but not photographic works of art) released before 1966 are in the public domain. See Commons:Copyright_rules_by_territory#Finland for details.

If you think this picture should be considered a work of art, add {{Delete-PD-Finland50}} to this page.

Äldre frimärken[edit]

  • {{PD-FinlandStamp}} – frimärken utgivna före den 1 januari 1990
Public domainPublic domainfalsefalse
This postage stamp published by Posti- ja telelaitos before January 1, 1990 is in the public domain because it is a part of a decision issued by a public authority in Finland. Section 9 of the Finnish Copyright Act specifies that no copyright exists in such material. (Link to the Act)

This template does not apply to postage stamps first published after 1989. Because Posti- ja telelaitos was changed from an authority to a company in the beginning of 1990, postage stamps published by Posti- ja telelaitos were not anymore part of decisions by an authority. Most probably the copyright owner of postage stamps published after 1989 are Itella Oyj or the designer of them. All postage stamps first published after 1989 marked with this tag should be speedily deleted.

  • {{PD-Kansallisarkisto}} – för arkivmaterial från Riksarkivets digitala arkiv.
Public domainPublic domainfalsefalse
This file has been downloaded from the digital archives of the Finnish National Archives Service (Kansallisarkisto). The Finnish National Archives Service states [2] that there are no copyright or privacy protection restrictions for the use of the files in its public archives database. The files here may be Public domain either due to age or due to their being official statements or decisions of Finnish authorities as per 9§ of the Finnish copyright law.

However, the National Archives Service asks that when material from the National Archives Services is used, the following information are given [3]:

  • The name of the archive unit holding the original material
  • The archive or collection where the material is kept
  • The unit code or name of material within the collection
