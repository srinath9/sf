Category for pronunciation. Please also add the pronunciation to an appropriate topic.

Suggested naming convention for pronunciation (words are case-sensitive, so please do not capitalize):

language code-word or phrase.ogg

for example, en-evolution.ogg, de-Eröffnung.ogg or ru-говорить.ogg.
